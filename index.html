Health & Wellness

Evidence-Based Health Tips

Practical advice for maintaining optimal health and wellness

Cardiovascular Health

Regular aerobic exercise, heart-healthy diet, stress management, and regular check-ups maintain optimal heart function.

Mental Wellness

Practice mindfulness, maintain social connections, prioritize sleep, and seek professional support when needed.

Physical Fitness

Combine cardio, strength training, and flexibility exercises. Aim for 150 minutes of moderate activity weekly.

Nutrition Balance

Eat diverse fruits and vegetables, stay hydrated, limit processed foods, and practice mindful eating habits.

Sleep Hygiene

Maintain consistent sleep schedule, create restful environment, limit screen time, and aim for 7-9 hours nightly.

Respiratory Care

Avoid pollutants, practice deep breathing, maintain indoor air quality, and engage in regular cardiovascular exercise.
